Video Games

My interest in video games has spawned when I was a very young child beginning with a certain Italian plumber character on a journey to save a princess.

In more recent years gaming has become a large industry in which I have become more interested in actually entering. I am inspired by the craftsmanship and talent put into one game and I wish to develop something that others may see.

My favourite video game series are "UNCHARTED", by Naughty Dog, for its beautiful graphics and enthralling narratives, and "The Legend of Zelda" series from Nintendo, for its fluid gameplay and immersive world.
